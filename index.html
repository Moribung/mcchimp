How It Works
01 / Answer
Every Fight is a Question

You pick answers under a countdown timer. Nail it fast — you win by KO. Scrape by and you go the distance. Miss completely and you get stopped.

02 / Climb
Build a Real Career

Start unranked in the regional circuit. Win fights, climb the rankings, earn promotion. Your record is a direct reflection of your knowledge.

03 / Adapt
Questions Learn From You

Questions you get right become easier over time. Questions you miss get harder. The game calibrates dynamically — always the right challenge.
